# Life Domain Review

## Live Tool
**https://beliefmastery.github.io/site/coaching.html**

## What This Tool Is

**Life Domain Review: Obstacles and Satisfaction Domains Mapping**

A self-assessment tool based on Sovereign of Mind frameworks that maps 15 Obstacles to Sovereignty and 10 Satisfaction Domains to identify highest-impact areas for focused work.

## Description

Review your life domains and satisfaction areas based on Sovereign of Mind frameworks. Complete comprehensive questionnaires to identify highest-impact areas for focused work.

## Key Features

- Maps 15 Obstacles to Sovereignty
- Evaluates 10 Satisfaction Domains
- Identifies highest-impact areas for focused work
- Generates personalized coaching profile
- Export results as JSON/CSV for AI integration

## How to Use

1. Visit https://beliefmastery.github.io/site/coaching.html
2. Complete the obstacle assessment
3. Complete the satisfaction domain evaluation
4. Review your personalized results
5. Export results as JSON/CSV (optional)
