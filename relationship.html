Scale Guide: Use the 0-10 scale to rate your relationship experience for each compatibility point.

  • 0-2 (Very Weak): Significant problems, frequent conflict, or major misalignment in this area.
  • 3-4 (Weak): Notable issues, occasional conflict, or clear areas for improvement.
  • 5-6 (Moderate): Some challenges, but generally functional with room for growth.
  • 7-8 (Strong): Generally good alignment with minor areas for improvement.
  • 9-10 (Very Strong): Excellent alignment, strong compatibility, minimal issues.

Be honest: Accurate assessment leads to more effective strategies. Consider both your experience and your partner's perspective when possible.
