## Archetype Characteristics Summary

### Dominant Leadership Archetypes
- **Alpha**: Natural authority, charisma, hierarchical leadership
- **Alpha-Xi**: Courageous protector, tactical leadership under pressure
- **Alpha-Rho**: Justice-oriented, fair authority, ethical leadership
- **Dark Alpha**: Tyrannical dominance, exploitative control

### Supportive/Subordinate Archetypes
- **Beta**: Reliable support, dutiful execution, system maintainer
- **Beta-Iota**: Gentle nurturer, innocent supporter, pure-hearted helper
- **Delta**: Hardworking laborer, non-leadership execution
- **Delta-Mu**: Nurturing father figure, protective provider

### Intellectual/Independent Archetypes
- **Gamma**: Brilliant outsider, rebellious intellectual, norm-defier
- **Gamma-Nu**: Romantic idealist, emotional artist
- **Gamma-Theta**: Mystic prophet, spiritual visionary
- **Gamma-Pi**: Opportunistic gambler, fortune-seeker
- **Dark Gamma**: Nihilistic seer, isolated truth-teller

### Autonomous/Lone Wolf Archetypes
- **Sigma**: Self-sufficient lone wolf, independent authority
- **Sigma-Kappa**: Strategic manipulator, political schemer
- **Sigma-Lambda**: Solitary artist, detached creator
- **Dark Sigma-Zeta**: Revolutionary anti-establishment fighter

### Marginalized Archetypes
- **Omega**: Social reject, passive loner, disconnected
- **Dark Omega**: Destructive consumer, entropic force

### Transcendent Archetype
- **Phi**: Fully ascended beyond society, enlightened being

## Usage Notes

**Important Considerations:**
1. These mappings are probabilistic, not deterministic
2. Individual aptitudes may override archetype tendencies
3. Market conditions affect viability of archetype-aligned careers
4. Personal development can shift archetype expression
5. Dark archetypes indicate potential dysfunction, not destiny
6. Multiple archetype expressions possible in one individual

**Recommended Application:**
- Use as one input factor (15-20% weight) in career matching
- Validate against measured aptitudes and skills
- Consider as work style preference indicator
- Flag major mismatches (e.g., Omega + high-social requirement)
- Provide development paths for archetype evolution
