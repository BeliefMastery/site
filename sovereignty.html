AI Sovereignty Analysis

"The sovereign split is underway. Let's use the 80/20 rule: 80% will queue up to be Borgified - liberated from the hardship of independent thought. Of the remaining 20%, the sovereign inclined - 80% of them will choose comfort over the hardship just to get the economic relief that comes from being optimized. That leaves only 4%, almost all of which will have to be religious about their sovereignty in order to resist the ever more seductive nature of the machine. If you are reading this then you are part of the 20% sovereign inclined... but that doesn't mean you can resist the compromise for comfort."

Are you part of the 4%? Or are you compromising sovereignty for comfort?

This self-assessment tool helps you understand your vulnerability to AI dependency and identity drift. The engine analyzes your usage patterns, attachment styles, and cognitive risks to position you within the "sovereign split" framework.

What you'll discover:

  • Your Cognitive Band: Where you fall in the IQ/cognitive complexity spectrum (based on thinking patterns, not formal testing)
  • Your Subclass: Specific patterns within your band (e.g., "Fluency Addict," "Mirror Loop Risk," "Sovereignty Core")
  • Your Attachment Mode: How you relate to AI (Tool, Companion, Authority, or Independent)
  • Your Vulnerability Risks: Top 5 areas where you're most at risk for dependency or identity drift
  • Your Sovereign Split Position: Are you in the 80% Queue, 16% Compromising, or 4% Core?
  • Your Sovereignty Score: 0-100 rating of your independence capacity
  • Personalized Action Plan: Specific counter-structures to maintain or build sovereignty

Assessment Structure: This multi-dimensional evaluation consists of 4 sections covering Usage Patterns, Cognitive Style, Attachment & Relationship, and Sovereignty Indicators. Estimated time: 20-30 minutes. All computation happens client-side for privacy - no data is sent to servers.
