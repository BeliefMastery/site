Find better dating success with attraction management

This honest and confronting assessment system identifies your strengths and weaknesses - supporting your direct confrontation and optimization.

Extended Explanation

Descriptive Framework: This assessment uses a three-cluster evolutionary psychology framework to analyze attraction dynamics. Based on red-pill theory and behavioral science, it evaluates how your traits signal value in mating markets through Coalition Rank, Reproductive Confidence, and Axis of Attraction dimensions.

This multi-tier assessment identifies your attraction profile through three comprehensive phases:

Male Assessment Path:

  • Phase 1 - Coalition Rank (3C's): Evaluates your standing among male peers through Courage, Control, and Competence - determining your access to resources and alliances.
  • Phase 2 - Reproductive Confidence (4P's): Assesses Perspicacity, Protector capacity, Provider capability, and Parental Investment - traits women evaluate for long-term mate selection.
  • Phase 3 - Axis of Attraction: Measures Performance/Status Signals (wealth, productivity, generosity) and Physical/Genetic Signals (aesthetics, fitness, vitality) that create immediate draw.

Female Assessment Path:

  • Phase 1 - Coalition Rank (3S's): Evaluates Social Influence, Selectivity & Mate Guarding Success, and Status Signaling - determining your position in female hierarchies.
  • Phase 2 - Reproductive Confidence: Assesses Paternity Certainty signals, Nurturing Standard, and Collaborative Trust Efficiency - traits men evaluate for long-term investment.
  • Phase 3 - Axis of Attraction: Measures Fertility & Health Cues (youth, beauty, vitality) and Risk Cost Indicators (stability, loyalty, drama risk) that influence male mate choice.
