How IDMC Solves Jobsite Problems

We orient every feature to one thing: reduce cost and time on the job. Below — clear problems and the on-site solutions IDMC provides.

Stop Paying Dump Fees

Landfill fees in NZ commonly exceed $266/tonne. Screen on-site to separate sellable aggregates and topsoil — avoid the dump and recover material value.

  • Processes 15–45 m³/hr (model dependent)
  • Creates 3 sellable grades: topsoil, 10mm, 20mm
  • Payback illustrated in ROI section

Cut Sorting Labour & Delays

Manual sorting costs hours of crew time. DeSite screeners are excavator-ready — one operator, vibration-free screening, fewer delays.

  • No onboard engine on many models — less maintenance
  • Portable & towable — works where you work
  • Reduces on-job sorting labour by up to 60%

Handle Contaminated & Mixed Soil

Quickly isolate fines and potential contaminants, reducing compliance risk and avoiding project hold-ups.

  • Fast separation keeps projects moving
  • Ideal for tight access Tasman / Nelson sites
  • Integrates with telehandler bins & attachments

Create Revenue from Waste

Turn previously discarded material into graded product for sale — local roads, garden supplies, or drainage metal.

  • Proven Australian pilots: revenue uplift 15–25%
  • On-farm grading reduces transport costs
  • Sell to local traders or use in next job

Quick Operation Steps
  1. Park screener in position and hook to excavator or tow vehicle.
  2. Feed material directly from the bucket — screen separates fines and aggregates.
  3. Collect sorted grades into bins or stockpiles for reuse or sale.
  4. Move to next location: compact, towable, fast.
Demo script (for sales staff): 1) Introduce cost problem (dump fees) — show local landfill price. 2) Run a 90-second screening — show product grades. 3) Calculate immediate job savings and resale potential. 4) Close: offer trial/demo day and delivery options.
