Your Sovereignty Paradigm

Discover the technical title that identifies your sovereignty paradigm. Which philosophical, spiritual, or psychological framework best describes your approach to sovereignty?

This tool clarifies which of 25 distinct philosophical, spiritual, and psychological paradigms best identifies your sovereignty framework. It assesses how well your actions align with your values, identifies derailing forces (hypocrisy, reluctance, nihilism), and provides the technical title that describes your sovereignty paradigm.

Extended Explanation

Assessment Purpose: The goal is identification and clarification, not ranking paradigms as superior or inferior.

This assessment evaluates:

  • Paradigm Rating: Rate all 25 paradigms on how much they resonate with you using a continuous 0-100 slider
  • Automatic Selection: System selects your top 3-5 paradigms based on your ratings to identify which frameworks best describe your approach
  • Practical Integration: How consistently you live according to your stated values within your identified paradigm
  • Derailer Detection: Hypocrisy (value-action gaps), reluctance (inertial resistance), and nihilism (despair-induced abdication) that may undermine your paradigm alignment
  • Paradigm Identification: Provides the technical title that best describes your sovereignty paradigm based on your responses
  • Remediation Paths: Recommended corrective paths based on your specific derailers
  • Export: JSON/CSV output for AI integration

Note: This tool illuminates patterns and provides diagnostic clarity. It is not a substitute for professional coaching or therapy. Results reflect your current organization of meaning, not a fixed identity.

Sovereignty Through-Line: This engine strengthens self-authorship, consent, and structural clarity. It is designed to increase agency, not dependence.

Methodology

  • Belief Mastery: Belief architecture, pattern recognition, calibrated change.
  • Sovereign of Mind: Sovereignty, consent, and authorship in decision-making.
  • Distortion Codex: Distortion detection, reality testing, and corrective clarity.
