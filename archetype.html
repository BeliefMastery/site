Modern Archetype Identification

Identify your primary, secondary, and tertiary modern archetypes. Discover your behavioral patterns and social role alignment!

This multi-tier assessment system identifies your dominant archetype through four phases of behavioral analysis.

Extended Explanation

Descriptive Framework: This assessment maps behavioral patterns and social roles. Archetypes are fluid; people contain multitudes. No archetype is superior to another. Results reflect current patterns, not fixed identity.

This multi-tier assessment system identifies your dominant archetype through four phases:

  • Phase 1 - Core Orientation: Identifies your primary archetype group (Alpha, Beta, Gamma, Delta, Sigma, Omega, or Phi) through concrete behavioral scenarios.
  • Phase 2 - Dimensional Refinement: Distinguishes between main types and their variants (e.g., Alpha vs Alpha-Xi vs Dark Alpha) through detailed behavioral statements.
  • Phase 3 - Shadow & Integration: Reveals situational variability, stress responses, and potential shadow expressions.
  • Phase 4 - Validation: Confirms archetype resonance through narrative scenarios.

Detailed Features

  • Primary, secondary, and tertiary archetype identification
  • Behavioral pattern mapping across 7 core groups (Alpha, Beta, Gamma, Delta, Sigma, Omega, Phi)
  • Subtype identification (e.g., Alpha-Xi, Gamma-Theta, Dark variants)
  • Shadow pattern detection for stress responses and integration opportunities
  • Aspirational trait identification for developmental growth
  • Status/Selection/Attraction markers extension
  • Plain-language explanations of archetype names and functions
  • Export functionality for AI integration

Based on modern red-pill archetype taxonomy with behavioral focus, concrete scenarios, and values-neutral framing.

Note: This assessment uses gender-neutral behavioral patterns. Archetypes apply regardless of gender identity, focusing on behavioral patterns and social roles rather than biological sex.
