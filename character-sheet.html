Astrology Character Sheet Generator

Transform your astrological profile into a D&D-style character sheet! This fun tool combines Western, Chinese, and Mayan astrology to create a unique roleplay character based on your birth chart.

Your character sheet includes Race, Class, Base Stats, Proficiencies, Traits, Saving Throws, Skills, Class Features, and Character Flaws—all derived from your astrological influences.

Note: For the most accurate character sheet, you'll need your Sun, Moon, and Ascendant signs from Western astrology. The Sun sign can be auto-calculated from your birth date, but Moon and Ascendant require your exact birth time and location. You can get these from astro.com or similar astrology calculators.
