Astrology Character Sheet Generator

Transform your astrological profile into a D&D-style character sheet! This fun tool combines Western, Chinese, and Mayan astrology to create a unique roleplay character based on your birth chart.

Your character sheet includes Race, Class, Base Stats, Proficiencies, Traits, Saving Throws, Skills, Class Features, and Character Flaws—all derived from your astrological influences.

Extended Explanation

Note: For the most accurate character sheet, you'll need your Sun, Moon, and Ascendant signs from Western astrology. The Sun sign can be auto-calculated from your birth date, but Moon and Ascendant require your exact birth time and location. You can get these from astro.com or similar astrology calculators. Use the astrology cheat sheet for the combined calculation logic behind this generator.

Detailed Features

  • Western Astrology: Sun, Moon, and Ascendant signs influence character stats
  • Chinese Astrology: Animal and Element determine Race and additional modifiers
  • Mayan Astrology: Seal and Tone provide unique abilities and approach styles
  • D&D-style character sheet with Race, Class, Base Stats, Proficiencies, Traits, Saving Throws, Skills, Class Features, and Flaws
  • Automatic Sun sign calculation from birth date
  • Built-in Moon and Ascendant calculator (birth time, timezone, latitude, longitude)
  • Export character sheet as JSON

Perfect for social roleplay games where you play as yourself with the powers of your astrological character.
