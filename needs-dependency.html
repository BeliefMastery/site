Identify dependency loops through comprehensive needs and vices assessment.

This system guides you through questions about surface experiences, needs, vices, and dependency patterns to identify how unmet needs create dependency loops. Based on Chapter 5: Needs and Beliefs from Belief Mastery, this tool helps you trace symptoms to root needs and break dependency cycles.

The assessment covers:

  • Surface Experiences: Irritability, withdrawal, conflict, isolation, and other surface symptoms
  • Need Identification: Mapping experiences to specific unmet needs using the proprietary needs vocabulary
  • Vice Identification: Recognizing vice expressions that signal unmet needs
  • Dependency Patterns: Identifying attraction-compulsion and aversion-avoidance patterns

Understanding the Scale (0-10):

  • 0-2 (Not at all / Never): The statement does not apply to you at all.
  • 3-4 (Minimal / Rarely): The statement occurs occasionally but has minimal impact.
  • 5-6 (Moderate / Sometimes): The statement is present with moderate frequency or intensity.
  • 7-8 (Significant / Often): The statement occurs frequently or with significant intensity.
  • 9-10 (Extreme / Always): The statement is nearly constant or extremely intense.

Guidelines:

  • Answer based on your experience in the relationship(s) you're analyzing
  • Be honest - recognition is the first step to protection
  • Consider how each pattern affects your autonomy and well-being
  • You can go back and change answers using the "Previous" button
